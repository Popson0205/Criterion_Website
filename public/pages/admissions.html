Admission Requirements

We admit students into JSS1 and SS1 each academic session. Placement in higher classes is subject to availability and assessment.

📋 JSS1 Entry Requirements

  • Primary School Leaving Certificate
  • Birth Certificate or Declaration of Age
  • 2 recent passport photographs
  • Completed application form
  • Entrance examination (conducted on-site)

📋 SS1 Entry Requirements

  • JSS3 Result / BECE Certificate
  • School Leaving Certificate from previous school
  • Birth Certificate or Declaration of Age
  • 2 recent passport photographs
  • Completed application form

Application Timeline

Step 1 — Apply Online

Complete the online application form. You will receive a reference number immediately.

Step 2 — Entrance Assessment

Shortlisted applicants are invited for a written assessment on campus.

Step 3 — Admission Decision

Results are communicated within 2 weeks. Check status online using your reference number.

Step 4 — Enrolment

Accepted students pay the acceptance fee and submit original documents to complete enrolment.
