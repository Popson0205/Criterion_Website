School Structure

Junior & Senior Secondary

We offer a six-year secondary programme from JSS1 through SS3, following the national curriculum framework.

Junior Secondary School (JSS 1–3)

The junior school provides a broad, balanced foundation across all subject areas. Students are introduced to core disciplines while developing study habits and critical thinking.

English Language Mathematics Basic Science Social Studies Civic Education Computer Studies French Fine Arts

Senior Secondary School (SS 1–3)

Students choose from Science, Commercial, or Arts tracks, preparing for WAEC and NECO examinations. All tracks include compulsory English, Mathematics, and Civic Education.

Science: Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Further Maths, Geography
Commercial: Economics, Accounting, Commerce, Government, Business Studies
Arts: Literature, Government, History, CRS/IRS, French, Fine Arts

Assessment

How We Assess Students

📝 Continuous Assessment (40%)

Class tests, assignments, projects, and practicals conducted throughout each term.

📋 Terminal Examinations (60%)

End-of-term examinations covering all topics taught during the session.

📊 Result Reports

Detailed result sheets with subject scores, class position, and teacher comments — available on the student portal.
