Our Story

Founded on a Vision

Criterion College was founded in 1998 with a single conviction: that every Nigerian child deserves an education that develops both the mind and the character. From a modest beginning with 120 students and 12 staff, we have grown into one of the region's most respected secondary schools.

Our name reflects our standard. "Criterion" — a benchmark, a measure of quality. That word drives every decision we make, from the teachers we hire to the programmes we run.

Today, over 1,200 students from across the state call Criterion College home, and our alumni hold positions of leadership in medicine, law, engineering, public service, and business across Nigeria and the world.

Mission, Vision & Values

🎯 Our Mission

To provide a rigorous, inclusive, and values-driven education that equips every student with the knowledge, skills, and character to succeed in a changing world.

🌟 Our Vision

To be the leading secondary school in the region, recognised for producing graduates who are academically excellent, morally upright, and nationally competitive.

⚖️ Our Values

Excellence — we set high standards.
Integrity — we act with honesty.
Service — we give back to our community.
Respect — we honour every person.
